How to play:

  1. There are 16 cards on the board, with 8 matching pairs of images hidden beneath them
  2. Flip your first card to see what is underneath
  3. Flip a second card, try to match and pictures
  4. The more you remember, the less incorrect matches you will make
  5. When you win, the game will remember your high score, so you can try and beat it
  6. Good luck!
